The MIT License grants rights to the *code*, not to the *brand*.

If you fork or redistribute LibCard, please:

- **Use a different name** for your distribution. Don't call your fork "LibCard"

  it is the official LibCard project or is endorsed by it.

  portions of the code (this is the one thing MIT actually requires).

You're welcome — encouraged, even — to build your own link-in-bio product on top
of this code. Just ship it under your own name.

Questions about use of the LibCard name? Open an issue or reach out to the
maintainer.
